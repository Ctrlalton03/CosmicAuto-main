About Us

Cosmic Auto Detailing is a family-owned business that has been serving the Greater Toronto Area for over 20 years. We are committed to providing the highest quality detailing services for your vehicle. Our team of professionals is dedicated to ensuring that your vehicle looks its best. We offer a wide range of services to meet your needs, from basic washes to full detailing packages. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and provide you with the best service possible. Contact us today to learn more about our services and to schedule an appointment.
